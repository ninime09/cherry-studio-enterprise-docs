**Estimated time: 5 minutes** — no deployment, sign up and go

<h3>Step 1: Register</h3>

Visit the Cherry Studio Enterprise website and register your admin account using your phone number or email. Enter the verification code and set your password, then click "Sign up".

<img src="/assets/images/en/express/register.webp" alt="Register" />

<h3>Step 2: Create an Organization</h3>

After registration, the system will guide you to create your organization. Fill in your **organization name**, select your **industry** and **company size**, then click "Create Organization".

<img src="/assets/images/en/express/create-org.webp" alt="Create organization" />

<h3>Step 3: Select a Plan</h3>

Choose a subscription plan that fits your needs. Click "Activate Now" to proceed.

<img src="/assets/images/en/express/select-plan.webp" alt="Select a plan" />

<Callout type="info">
  [View full pricing →](/docs/pricing)
</Callout>

<h3>Step 4: Enterprise Verification</h3>

Before activation, you need to complete enterprise verification. The flow has three steps — follow the on-screen instructions:

**1. Upload business license** — supports JPG and PNG (max 5MB). After uploading, the system will OCR the company name, unified social credit code, and legal representative — no manual entry required.

<img src="/assets/images/en/express/enterprise-verify-1.webp" alt="Upload business license" />

**2. Upload the legal representative's ID card** — upload the **front side** and **back side** separately. The system will automatically recognize the name and ID number.

<img src="/assets/images/en/express/enterprise-verify-2.webp" alt="Upload legal representative ID" />

**3. Submit for review** — confirm all information is correct, then click "Submit for Review". Once approved, the service will be activated.

<img src="/assets/images/en/express/enterprise-verify-3.webp" alt="Submit for review" />

<h3>Step 5: Payment & Activation</h3>

Once enterprise verification is approved, you'll be taken to the order summary page. Confirm the order details, read and check the **Service Agreement** and **Compliance Commitment**, then click "Submit Order".

<img src="/assets/images/en/express/payment-1.webp" alt="Order summary with discount code entry" />

<Callout type="info">
Have a discount code? Enter it in the **Discount code** field on the order summary and click **Apply**. See [Using a Discount Code](/docs/admin/order-management/using-discount-code).
</Callout>

In the payment method dialog that appears, choose how to pay:

- **Alipay** — instant scan-to-pay, suited to individuals and small teams
- **Bank transfer** — suited to organizations requiring invoices

<img src="/assets/images/en/express/payment-2.webp" alt="Select payment method" />

After payment, the system will automatically deploy and activate the service for your organization. This usually completes within a few minutes.

<h3>Step 6: Invite Members</h3>

Go to "User Management" in the admin console and click "Invite Members" to generate an invite link. You can:

- Assign members to a specific **group** (e.g., Operations, Engineering)
- Set the invite link's **expiration time** and **max usage count**
- Share the link with your team — they click it to register and automatically join the assigned group

<Callout type="info">
If you haven't created any groups yet, go to [Create & Edit Groups](/docs/admin/group-management/create-and-edit-groups) to set them up first.
</Callout>

<img src="/assets/images/en/express/invite-members.webp" alt="Invite members" />

<Callout type="info">
  [View full member invitation docs →](/docs/admin/user-management/invite-members)
</Callout>
